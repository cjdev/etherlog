a backlog tool for agile teams

TeamStory is a tool that helps agile teams ...
  • maintain a backlog
  • communicate with stakeholders
  • retrospect

agile teams

Agile teams value communication over tools and process; They embrace change. They are constantly improving.

But agilists are mortals. They are good at communicating, good at adapting process to the present, but not good at remembering and number-crunching.

TeamStory takes this to heart, eschewing features that limit project flexibility, focusing instead on making it easy to see the truth and communicate it.

maintain a backlog

The backlog is owned by the whole team. Product owners and team members work together to formulate vague epics, then the team estimates them. It's always just a silly guess at first.

But, over time, the team works through the project. Epics are broken-down into more granular stories. Estimates are honed. The team gets closer to the truth, the final outcome takes shape.

TeamStory makes it easy to quickly take-down and prioritize epics, make some silly guesses, and work from there.

communicate with stakeholders

Agile teams adore their stakeholders. They want them to get frequent and honest project updates, so that the project stays responsive to their needs.

TeamStory makes it easy to see what has changed since the last time a stakeholder has received an update, freeing teams to focus on communicating, collaborating and innovating.

retrospect

Agile Teams regularly set aside time to look back at their recent performance.

TeamStory was built from the ground up to store and replay the complete history of a project, freeing teams to focus on the why instead of the what
